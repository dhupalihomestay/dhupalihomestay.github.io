About Dhupali Homestay - Your Perfect Karnataka Getaway

Dhupali Homestay is a locally owned and operated homestay dedicated to providing travelers with a home away from home! Located beside the sacred Sri 1008 Bhagawan Shantinatha Digambara temple in Samse, Kalasa, we offer an authentic and memorable experience in the heart of Karnataka's natural beauty.

Nature & Serenity: Experience our serene retreat nestled amidst lush Western Ghats greenery! Immerse yourself in the lap of nature, surrounded by picturesque landscapes and the melodious chirping of birds. It's the perfect escape from city life for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

Authentic Karnataka Cuisine: Indulge your taste buds in a culinary journey with our freshly prepared traditional Karnataka meals. From local Malnad delicacies to comfort food, our menu (Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner) is designed to tantalize your senses and provide you with an authentic taste of Karnataka's rich culinary heritage.

Adventure & Trekking Tours: Embark on thrilling trekking adventures and captivating sightseeing tours in the Western Ghats, all conveniently located near our homestay. Our expert local guides will accompany you on exciting trails, unveiling hidden gems, breathtaking viewpoints, and cascading waterfalls of the Chikmagalur region.
